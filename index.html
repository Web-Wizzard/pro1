Whiteface Lodge

Wide array of amenities to make your time enjoyable and relaxing



Whiteface Lodge is a luxurious, rustic resort located in Lake Placid, New York, nestled in the Adirondack Mountains. The lodge is known for its combination of elegant accommodations and a charming, old-world atmosphere. Inspired by traditional Adirondack architecture, it features grand wooden beams, stone fireplaces, and a warm, inviting interior that blends seamlessly with the natural surroundings.







Welcome


Nestled in the heart of the majestic Adirondacks, Whiteface Lodge offers an unparalleled experience where rustic charm meets luxurious comfort. Whether you're here for outdoor adventure, a peaceful retreat, or simply to unwind in style, our lodge provides the perfect setting for all. Surrounded by pristine nature, you’ll find a wide array of activities to suit every interest—from skiing and hiking to boating and fishing.






Main Features







Accommodations

The lodge offers spacious, well-appointed rooms and suites that provide a cozy, upscale ambiance with natural wood finishes and a rustic mountain feel. Guests can enjoy luxurious amenities


Dining Experiences

The lodge offers exceptional dining options, including fine dining at the "The Lobby Lounge" and casual meals at "Kanu", where guests can enjoy gourmet dishes with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients in a cozy and refined setting.


Private Lakefront

The lodge boasts a private lakefront area on Lake Placid, where guests can enjoy beautiful views, boat rentals, and beach activities. It's an ideal spot for water recreation or simply relaxing by the shore.






ROOMS






The rooms at Whiteface Lodge are a perfect blend of rustic elegance and modern luxury, offering guests a cozy yet sophisticated retreat. Each room is thoughtfully designed with warm wood accents, stone fireplaces, and spacious layouts that exude a lodge-style charm. Whether you opt for a one-bedroom suite, a multi-bedroom cabin, or a luxury lodge room, every space is equipped with high-end amenities, including fully stocked kitchens, plush bedding, and private balconies with scenic views of Lake Placid or the surrounding Adirondack Mountains.















Whiteface lodge

Evoking the Gilded Age splendor of the historic Adirondack Great Camps in its rustic timber design, Whiteface Lodge is tucked into the picturesque woodlands of the Adirondacks’ High Peaks region in Upstate New York. The village of Lake Placid, home to two Winter Olympic Games and a wealth of year-round attractions, lies within walking distance. And surrounding it all, six million spectacular mountain acres of Adirondack Park—the largest protected area in the U.S. mainland. The breathtaking variety of geography provides plenty of outdoor activities. However, on-site amenities are plentiful as well, making the lodge one of the most alluring Lake Placid resorts.

Whiteface Lodge is a luxurious resort nestled in the scenic Adirondack Mountains of Lake Placid, New York. This rustic-chic retreat blends the charm of a traditional Adirondack lodge with modern comforts and upscale amenities. Surrounded by stunning natural beauty, the lodge offers an array of year-round activities, including skiing and snowboarding in winter, as well as hiking, kayaking, and fishing during the warmer months.
